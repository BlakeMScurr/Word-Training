This tool trains you to recognise words instantly and is aimed at students who are learning to read or struggling with dyslexia.

The computer will say a word, and you select the word on the page you have heard.

You can choose a word by clicking on it or using the numbers 1, 2, and 3 on your keyboard.

Once you've recognised each word correctly 3 times in a row, you are finished.
